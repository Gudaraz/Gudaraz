### 👨‍💻 About Me
I am a **Licensed Attorney and Software Developer** specializing in legal operations transformation, process automation, and legal technology implementation. Currently working as an **Independent Legal Operations & Legal Tech Consultant** in Europe, I design and build systems—from workflow architecture and automated document generation to proprietary RAG deployments—bridging legal and technical teams through the full implementation cycle.

🗣️ **Languages:** Spanish (Native) | English (C1) | Russian (B2)

<br>

### 🏆 Key Milestones & Impact
- 🏅 **Award-Winning Innovation:** Designed and developed **Younomad**, a legal settlement platform for digital nomads, winning the **2025 Concurso de Innovación Jurídica** (€10,000 seed capital) in Madrid.
- ⚡ **80% Efficiency Boost:** Redesigned intestate succession workflows and wrote automation code at Total Abogados, cutting case processing from 60 to 15 minutes.
- 🧠 **Enterprise AI & RAG:** Developed full implementation designs for RAG-based compliance knowledge assistants for global corporations, ensuring strict adherence to **GDPR** and the **EU AI Act**.
- 📄 **CLM Development:** Co-developed **TAPP Legal**, a B2B Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) platform enabling enterprise HR departments to manage employment contracts independently.

<br>

### 🌱 Current Focus & Explorations
- **Full Stack Python Development:** Deepening my backend expertise through the Digital Talent Scholarship (2026).
- **AI Governance & RAG:** Advising on EU AI Act risk classification, GDPR operational requirements, and privacy-by-design architectures.
- **Process Design:** Translating complex legal requirements into engineering specifications (BPMN diagrams, IF/THEN logic, API blueprints).
